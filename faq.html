STIF (Sustainability Technology and Innovation Forum) is a social enterprise dedicated to democratizing sustainability for businesses of all sizes. We provide affordable tools, resources, and community support to help SMEs navigate their sustainability journey, track their ESG progress, and meet their environmental goals.
STIF membership is designed for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs), sustainability professionals, ESG consultants, and anyone committed to advancing sustainable business practices. Whether you're just starting your sustainability journey or looking to enhance your existing initiatives, STIF provides the tools and community support you need.
STIF offers a comprehensive suite of tools including: ESG Readiness Assessment Tool, GHG Scope 1 and 2 calculators (Scope 3 coming soon), Impact tracking and visualization tools, Guides and how-to resources, STIFfocuses sessions for deeper discussions, Knowledge-sharing community platform, and access to our catalogue of sustainability solutions.
The ESG Readiness Tool is an online assessment that evaluates your current ESG maturity level across environmental, social, and governance dimensions. It guides you through key sustainability questions, analyzes your responses, and provides a comprehensive report identifying your strengths, gaps, and priority areas for development. The tool integrates with our tracking modules to help you measure progress over time.
Yes, STIF provides tools and guidance to support your ESG reporting requirements. Our platform helps you collect, organize, and track the data needed for various reporting frameworks. We offer templates, best practice guides, and calculators aligned with common standards. While we provide the tools and knowledge, we also connect you with our community of experts who can offer additional support.
STIF operates on a tiered membership model designed to be affordable for SMEs. We offer free access to basic tools like our ESG Readiness Assessment and GHG Scope 1 & 2 calculators. Premium features, advanced analytics, and full community access are available through our paid subscription plans. As a social enterprise, all profits are reinvested to improve our platform and support the SME sustainability community.
Getting involved is easy! Sign up for a STIF account to access our community platform where you can participate in discussions, attend STIFfocuses sessions, share best practices, ask questions, and connect with peers facing similar sustainability challenges. Members can also contribute to our knowledge base, participate in working groups, and attend exclusive events.

As a social enterprise, affordability is central to our mission. We keep costs low through efficient operations, community-driven content creation, and a scalable digital platform. We offer flexible pricing tiers to match different business sizes and needs, with many essential tools available for free. All profits are reinvested rather than distributed to shareholders, allowing us to prioritize accessibility over maximum returns.
STIF is unique in combining affordable technology, expert guidance, and community support specifically for SMEs. Unlike expensive consultancy services or complex enterprise software, we provide an all-in-one platform designed for small business realities. As a social enterprise, we prioritize impact over profit. Our community-driven approach means you're learning alongside peers, sharing real-world solutions, and collectively advancing sustainability together.
