What is STIF?

Sustainability is crucial because it ensures we meet present needs without compromising future generations' ability to meet theirs. STIF makes sustainability meaningful by providing SMEs with affordable, practical tools and a supportive community to take concrete action on environmental, social, and governance issues.
Sustainable SMEs are shaping a future where businesses of all sizes contribute to a healthier planet and society. Through STIF, SMEs can access the tools and knowledge to reduce their environmental impact, improve social responsibility, and build resilient business models that thrive in an increasingly sustainability-focused economy.
Becoming an Eco-Ready SME means building your capacity to understand, measure, and improve your environmental impact. STIF helps you achieve this through our ESG Readiness Tool, impact calculators, educational resources, and community support. Start with our free assessment to identify where you are and create a roadmap for progress.
