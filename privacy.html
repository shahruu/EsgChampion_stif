STIF Privacy policy

Last Revised: April 17, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Sustainability Technology and Innovation Forum (STIF). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled with care under your personal data protection laws. This Privacy Policy will show how we handle the details of our personal data collection, use and information in accordance with our platform and services.

2. Data Controller

STIF is the data controller and is responsible for the your processing data. If you have any questions about this policy or our data processing practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer at [Contact Email].

3. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information about you:

  • Personal details: Name, contact details, job title, or company or similar details we included or agreed for our use or signups
  • Technical Data: Includes IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system, and other technology on the devices you use to access this website
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, products and services
  • Marketing and Communications Data: Your preferences in receiving marketing from us and your communication preferences

4. How We Use Your Personal Data

We will only use your personal data when in the law allows us to. Most commonly we use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To provide and improve our services, including processing registrations and managing your account
  • To personalize your experience and deliver content and features that match your interests
  • To communicate with you for transactional purposes (e.g. account updates, security, system performance)
  • To understand and analyze how you and our marketing campaigns and features are being communicated and use of our website
  • To notify you about significant changes to our service, including policy, terms or agreements, and related updates in our service offerings and use your details (either to help you contract performance and data, use for, perform, personalize, or further purposes)
  • To enforce terms of policies and to keep our platform and users safe

5. Sharing Your Personal Data

We may have to share your personal data with external bodies and contractors, such as: service providers who support our business (e.g. IT and system administration services, security providers), professional advisors (e.g. lawyers, bankers, auditors and insurers), government bodies that require us to report processing activities.

6. International Transfers

We may transfer your personal data outside the UK and the European Economic Area (EEA) or where data of transfer is adequately protected according to applicable law. In these cases, we ensure that transfers are made in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

7. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered or disclosed. In addition, we limit access to your personal data to those agents, contractors, and other third parties who have a business need to know. They will only process your personal data on our instructions and are subject to a duty of confidentiality.

8. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

9. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws, including the right to request access, correction, erasure, restriction, transfer, to object to processing, to portability of your personal data and (where we are relying on consent as a valid, or clause, to withdraw your consent to any legitimate use of personal data.

10. Marketing

You have the right to opt-out to our promotional communications are mailing messages (or marketing purposes. We will send you direct marketing, unsubscribe, advertising where you have given us your opt-in. We will always inform you before collecting your personal data if we intend to use your data for such purposes.

11. Changes to this Policy

Any changes we make to our Privacy policy in the future will be posted on this page. Please check back frequently to see any changes or updates to our privacy policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our processing please email us at [Your Contact Email].
